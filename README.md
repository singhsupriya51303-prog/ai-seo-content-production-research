# AI-Powered SEO Content Production Research

## Overview

This repository contains research on how leading SEO and content marketing practitioners use artificial intelligence to improve content production, optimization, and distribution.

The goal is to identify common workflows, best practices, and emerging patterns that can support the development of a practical AI-powered SEO playbook for B2B SaaS companies.

## Research Topic

AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Objectives

- Identify leading practitioners in AI-powered SEO
- Collect publicly available content from selected experts
- Analyze recurring strategies and workflows
- Document key insights and best practices
- Create a foundation for a future AI SEO playbook
